Lichuan Ren

I am a final-year PhD candidate in Industrial and Systems Engineering at Rutgers University, advised by Dr. Zhimin Xi.

My research focuses on developing algorithms that enhance the safety and reliability of intelligent systems, with a particular emphasis on uncertainty quantification and risk-aware control for autonomous driving systems.

Prior to my PhD, I earned a master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Rutgers University, working on robotics manipulation under the guidance of Dr. Jingjin Yu.
